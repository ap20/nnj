A New Miracle Material?

Just one atom thick, it is an allotrope of carbon arranged in a two-dimensional hexagonal (honeycomb) arrangement. It is 100 times stronger than the best steel, conducts heat and electricity, and is transparent. This "miracle substance" is being touted for a huge variety of potential applications, with robust research underway the world over.

It is a cousin to the "lead" in a common pencil and is more conductive than copper. This substance is also related to fullerenes and nanotubes. And here is an amazing feature of this substance...self-healing, when exposed to the right chemicals!

Know what it is ?

Graphene!

Here are some exciting possibilities, in a non-prioritized laundry list:

  • Light emitting devices to compete with conventional lighting systems
  • Carbon-based bionic devices
  • Electronic components [replacement for transistors?]/computer chips/touchscreens
  • Optoelectronic applications
  • Photovoltaic [solar-electric] panels
  • Ultra-strong composite materials
  • Battery systems
  • High power supercapacitors and storage batteries
  • Smart Paint
  • Filtration of drinking water from seawater
  • Body armor
  • Improved cement
  • Lubricating oils

As I write this, I see a TV commercial advertising this anti-aging application of a graphene based product to reduce face wrinkles and under eye bags! Now that is a wide ranging technology.
