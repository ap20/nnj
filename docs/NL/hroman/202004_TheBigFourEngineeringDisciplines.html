The Big Four Engineering Disciplines

Prism magazine, the flagship publication for the ASEE [American Society of Engineering Educators] disclosed in the January 2020 issue some interesting engineering statistics. The total number of engineers in the U.S. in 2019 was 2,019,300, and broken down in this way:

Electrical / Electronics Civil Mechanical Industrial
330,400 326,800 312,900 284,600

Of course there are many other engineering disciplines as well, probably in total over 100 identifiable disciplines; but these above are the big four disciplines. Let's look at the projections for 2028.

Civil Electrical / Electronics Mechanical Industrial
347,300 338,400 325,700 308,800

The total number of engineers projected to be here in 2028 is 2,115,100.
